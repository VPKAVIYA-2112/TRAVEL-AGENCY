Travel Agency Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information, such as your name, email address, and phone number, when you make a booking or contact us through our website.
  • Payment information, including credit card details, when you make a reservation.
  • Demographic information, such as your age, gender, and location, for statistical purposes.
  • Information about your preferences and interests to provide you with personalized travel recommendations.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process your bookings and provide the requested travel services.
  • To communicate with you regarding your reservations, inquiries, and customer support.
  • To personalize your experience and recommend tailored travel options.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer satisfaction.
  • To conduct market research and analyze trends.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and delivering our services.
  • Travel suppliers, such as airlines, hotels, and car rental companies, to fulfill your bookings.
  • Government authorities or law enforcement agencies as required by law.

We will not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.

Security

We implement security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access and misuse.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
