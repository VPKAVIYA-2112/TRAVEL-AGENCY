Australian Famous Places

Sydney Opera House

The Sydney Opera House is a multi-venue performing arts center located in Sydney, Australia. It is one of the most famous and distinctive buildings of the 20th century.

Great Barrier Reef

The Great Barrier Reef is the world's largest coral reef system composed of over 2,900 individual reefs and 900 islands. It is one of the seven wonders of the natural world.

Uluru (Ayers Rock)

Uluru, also known as Ayers Rock, is a large sandstone rock formation in the southern part of the Northern Territory in central Australia. It is sacred to the Indigenous Australians.
