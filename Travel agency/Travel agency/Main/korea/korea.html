Famous Places in South Korea

Gyeongbokgung Palace

Gyeongbokgung Palace, located in Seoul, is the largest and most iconic of the five grand palaces built during the Joseon Dynasty. It is renowned for its beautiful traditional Korean architecture and historical significance.

Bukchon Hanok Village

Bukchon Hanok Village is a traditional Korean village located in Seoul. It offers a glimpse into the city's past with its well-preserved hanok (traditional Korean houses), narrow alleys, and charming cultural atmosphere.

Jeju Island

Jeju Island is a volcanic island located off the southern coast of South Korea. It is a popular tourist destination known for its stunning natural landscapes, beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
